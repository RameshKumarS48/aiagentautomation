# Introduction to RAG Systems RAG systems, also known

as Red, Amber, and Green systems, are a powerful tool for businesses looking to streamline their operations and improve efficiency. These systems provide a simple yet effective way to categorize and prioritize tasks, allowing teams to focus on the most critical activities.
